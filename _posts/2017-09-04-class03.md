$$ \text{mean} (Y_i) = \alpha + \beta * X_i $$


$$ Y_i = \alpha + \beta * X_i + \epsilon_i, \quad mean(\epsilon_i) = 0 $$


$$ \alpha, \beta \in \arg\min \left\{ \left. \sum_i \left(y_i - a - b x_i \right)^2 \quad \right| \quad a, b \in \mathbb{R} \right\} $$
